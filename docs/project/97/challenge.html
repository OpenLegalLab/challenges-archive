Plyply - the Smart Compliance-Tool

Aufbau eines Compliance-tools, das als Copilot organisationsintern verwendet kann (und auf die Bedürfnisse der Orga. abgestimmt werden kann.

⛶  Fullscreen ↓  Download

The idea of ai as a co-pilot is gaining ground, and so the questino is: can we design a co-pilot that helps us through the compliance-jungle? Ideally, the challenge would find a very specific compliance theme to find out more about what the requirements for such a compliance bot would be.
